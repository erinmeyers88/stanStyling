A native of South Carolina, Stan Huff owned several homes throughout the state before entering the real estate business in 1999. His experience first as a homeowner, and second as a real estate agent, means that he understands both sides of the market. He enjoys showing the Charleston area to newcomers and is an expert at guiding both buyers and sellers in the Lowcountry region to successful sales.

Stan and his wife Kym raised their three children in Mount Pleasant, and love the acclaimed school systems and secure neighborhoods the area provides. The Huffs enjoy spending time at the beach and on their boat, where they can experience the history of Charleston from the unique perspective of the water.
